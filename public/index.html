Editorial principles

Less hand-waving.
More mental models.

Daedalus is an independent engineering journal for readers who want the machinery, not just the diagram.

  1. 01

    Make the invisible visible

    Requests, queues, storage, timing, and failure paths become diagrams you can reason about.

  2. 02

    Name the production tradeoff

    Every pattern earns its complexity. The journal tells you where it pays and where it does not.

  3. 03

    Leave with a reusable model

    The goal is not recall. It is a framework you can carry into the next design review.
